Broke Boi Bibimbap

Description

As the name suggests, this will be the broke-boi version of the bibimbap. DO NOT think that your bibimbap will look the same as the picture above. That too fancy, this the broke-boi version.

Ingredients

  • cooked rice
  • one egg
  • soy sauce
  • gochujang
  • sesame seed oil

Steps

  1. Fry your egg. If you rich and can afford more than one egg, fry up more. Watch your cholesterol though.
  2. Get a bowl and put your rice in the bowl.
  3. Add your egg on to the rice.
  4. Add your gochujang, soy sauce, and sesame seed oil.
  5. Mix.
  6. Enjoy.
