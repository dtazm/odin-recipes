Rice Cake

Description

This dish is a traditional Korean street food. The rice cake is made by pounding cooked rice till it becomes almost marshmellow-like texture. The rice cake is then cooked in a spicy sauce with additional ingredients added to the sauce. This dish can be enjoyed on the streets and in restaurants

Ingredients

  • eomuk
  • green cabbage
  • scallions
  • garlic
  • rice cake
  • tteokbokki sauce

Steps

  1. Soak the rice cakes for 20 minutes or longer.
  2. Cut the fish cake, cabbage, and scallions into 2-inch pieces.
  3. In a large pan, stir in the sauce into waterand bring it to a boil.
  4. Add the rice cakes and boil until the rice cakes become soft and the sauce is thickened (about 8-10 minutes, maybe longer), stirring occasionally.
  5. Add the remaining ingreidents. Continue to boil, stirring constantly, for an additional 4-6 minutes. Depending on rice cakes, you may need more time to reach a desired level of softness. Feel free to add more water as necessary.
