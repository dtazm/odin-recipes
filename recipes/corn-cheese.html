Corn Cheese

Description

A relatively simple dish. At its simplest form, this dish only requires 4 ingredients to make. This cheese and corn dish usually accompanies drinks in Korean bars. A simple, yet delicious dish that is quick and easy to make!

Ingredients

  • canned corn
  • butter
  • Japanese Kewpie mayo
  • mozzarella cheese (shredded)

Steps

  1. On a skillet, melt the butter till it coats the bottom of the skillet.
  2. Once the bottom of the skillet is covered in butter, pour in the corn after you drain most of the liquid. Stir untill the corn is even heated.
  3. Add in the mayo. If you would like a more creamy dish, add a lot of mayo and if not, add it sparingly.
  4. After stirring for 1-2 minutes and the mayo has warmed up, add in some of your mozarella cheese and stir.
  5. Add the rest of your cheese on top and wait till the cheese on top has melted. You may blow torch or put the skillet in an oven to speed this process up.
  6. Enjoy!
